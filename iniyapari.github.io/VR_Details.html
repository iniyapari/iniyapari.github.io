Title: Octavia

Dimensions: Unity Engine World Space

Year: 2022

Details: This artwork is based off one of the imagined worlds by Italo Calvino from his book "Imaginary Cities" This city in particular hangs between two cliffs that are hanging the civilization through spiderwebs. This artwork made me realize how fascinating Immersive Media was, and also how enriching and necessary it is to work in a team.
